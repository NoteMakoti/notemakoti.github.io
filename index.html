About Me

Part gym rat, part geek; I enjoy making websites that not only look good, but feel good to use.

The way I see it, there are too many websites nowadays that either look like great magazine spreads (while running slowly with janky scrolling), perform very well (while looking like a 4th grader’s first PowerPoint presentation), or just don’t look special at all (due to an overreliance on pre-made templates and website “generators” like Squarespace). A web developer’s job is to help promote a client’s message, and to make the user’s experience seamless and captivating. It requires empathy, and you won’t find that with something who automates the whole process for a quick buck.
