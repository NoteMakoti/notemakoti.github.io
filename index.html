About Me

My name is Stanley McCatty. I'm a self-taught front-end web developer with a passion for technology and design that makes life easier.

My hobbies are reading, gaming, and maintaining an obscene hoard of movies and music.

I grow the most when I'm given a challenge. The feeling you get when you're gnawing away at a problem until a project finally comes together is the best part of being a developer. If you have a Github project that can use a helping hand, feel free to drop me a line.
